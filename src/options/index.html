Canvas+ Options

Adds tabs to the top of the page for quickly switching between courses. Also hides the default course tab on Canvas

Change the order of your tabs

Use the arrows to move your courses up and down to reorder your tabs.

Dashboard

Adds the grades table to the dashboard for quick viewing

Adds upcoming assignments within one week

Adds tags to the top left of course cards that show the grade of that class

Custom Links

For each course, you can specific a link that is easily accessible from any page (such as a Zoom Link). It'll show up as a button on the top right of your page!
